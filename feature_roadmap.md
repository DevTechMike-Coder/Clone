# Project Feature Roadmap: Clone

Based on the current project structure (Expo with file-based routing), here is a curated list of features for your social media application.

## 👤 User Management & Profile

- **Authentication System**: Secure login, registration, and password recovery.
- **Social Login**: One-tap sign-in with Google or Apple.
- **Bio & Profile Editing**: Users can update their avatar, name, and bio.
- **Following System**: Ability to follow, unfollow users and see their posts.
- **Account Privacy**: Options for public vs private profiles.

## 📱 Content & Feed

- **Visual Feed**: A scrollable list of image and video posts with modern UI.
- **Post Creation**: Support for high-quality image uploads with filters or simple text posts.
- **Interactions**: Like, comment, and save posts to a personal collection.
- **Explore Page**: A discovery section for finding trending content and new accounts.
- **Stories**: Disappearing 24-hour video or image updates.

## 💬 Communication

- **Real-time Messaging**: Instant chat with friends using WebSockets or Firebase.
- **Direct Push Notifications**: Stay updated with likes, comments, and new messages even when the app is closed.
- **Media Sharing in Chat**: Send images and videos directly through direct messages.
- **Group Chats**: Create rooms for multiple users to talk simultaneously.

## 🔍 Discovery & Utility

- **Global Search**: Find users, hashtags, and locations quickly.
- **Activity Feed**: A dedicated tab for all notifications and recent interactions.
- **Rich Media Support**: Native performance for fast-loading images and smooth video playback.
- **Dark Mode**: A premium, OLED-friendly dark theme.

## 🛠️ Advanced Features (Premium/Scale)

- **Content Moderation**: Automated or manual system to flag and remove inappropriate content.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: See how many people viewed each post.
- **Multi-Account Support**: Switch between multiple profiles effortlessly.
- **Livestreaming**: Go live and interact with followers in real-time.
